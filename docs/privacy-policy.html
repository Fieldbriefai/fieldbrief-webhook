Information We Collect

When you use the FieldBrief SMS service, we collect your phone number and the content of text messages you send to our service number. We also collect job-related data you provide including job addresses, descriptions, and scheduling information.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide job management services via SMS, including creating and updating job records, dispatching technicians, and sending job status notifications. Your data is stored securely in our systems and is used solely for service delivery.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using industry-standard cloud services with encryption in transit and at rest. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

SMS Communications

By texting our service number, you consent to receive job-related SMS notifications. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by texting STOP. Text HELP for support information.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.
